    Here is a short GUIDE to help you get the most out of our service:

    Your account:

    We recommend using a personal email. While school emails are the highest preference for students, they may not be highly confidential like your .Gmail, .cloud, or .Yahoo email. Please message support or chat if you need us to edit and change your email from the school to personal.

    TIP: Using your personal email is highly confidential.
    Submitting a new task:

    When submitting a new task or order, you must provide the following: Assignment Type, Service Type, Deadline, Language, Number of Pages/Words, Education Level, Custom Instructions, and any files which are required to complete your order.?

    TIP: To get your order on time, we HIGHLY recommend setting the deadline AT LEAST a day before the actual deadline. Bidding phase:
    After you've filled out the required information and clicked "Submit Order", our tutors will bid on your order. Bidding is simply proposing the price for completing a task. To hire the best tutor for your needs, you should consider their: ratings, customer reviews, & expertise. Once you've decided, click "Pick Tutor" and deposit funds to your account.
    TIP: Please make sure your payment is valid and has sufficient funds.

    Order in Progress Phase:

    During this phase, you can chat or message your tutor to discuss your order, ask for updates, request drafts, or request edits on the draft. Once the final version has been uploaded, your order will automatically move to Completed orders.

    TIP: We offer FREE and unlimited adjustments! If necessary, you can request edits from your writer until you are 100% satisfied with the results.
    Approving the Order:

    Once you access the final version of the completed order, please double-check and proofread it. When you are 100% satisfied, click "Release Payment" and pay the writer for their efforts.

    TIP: We HIGHLY recommend you make minor edits to the solution to match your personal style. You Must also copy the order text or content and paste it into a new document on your computer. Every file contains "metadata" such as name, date, etc. By following this step, you'll become the ORIGINAL owner of the file.
    Support:
    Customer support is available 24/7 to answer any of your questions and help with your order!
    Tip from Support: Please be aware that you are solely responsible for studying all the materials provided by our tutors. They are essential for earning an academic degree and applying for your future job.
